==========
IMPRESSION
==========


Impression is a lite content manager that let's you write articles and publish them on your website.
Article can be added to one or more categories.


Why Lite?

Impression is lite because is it's very straight forward.
No special functions like notification, commenting, and rating are implemented.
The submit forms are very basic; give the title of the article, enter the first paragraph (for the spotlight block), and the rest of the article. Choose a category, time of publishing and submit your article.
